About

I am a PhD candidate in Earth Sciences at The Ohio State University studying submarine landslides, sediment transport, and marine geohazards. My work connects sedimentology, geomechanics, and computational approaches to understand slope instability, sediment routing, and mass transport deposits in convergent margin systems.

Research

My research focuses on submarine landslides, sediment transport, and marine geohazards, especially in systems such as the Japan Trench. I am interested in how tectonics, slope processes, and sediment properties interact to shape hazard and stratigraphic records.
